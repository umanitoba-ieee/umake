What is a Make·a·thon?

Hardware First

Unlike traditional hackathons, we provide sensors, microcontrollers, and tools to help you build physical devices that solve real-world problems.

Collaborate

Form a team of 2-4 and work alongside mentors from top tech companies to bring your wildest prototypes to life.

Win Big

Over $10,000 in hardware prizes, internships, and the chance to showcase your project to industry leaders.

2026 Themes

St. Patricks Day

Retro-Gaming

Build custom arcade machines, controllers, or physical games that bridge the gap between digital and physical play.

FAQ

Who can apply?

UMake is open to everyone! We welcome beginners and veterans alike!

What if I don't have hardware?

No problem! We have a dedicated hardware lab where you can sign out everything from Arduinos to 3D printers.

What is the registration fee?

Registration, hardware, and building materials are free of cost. Those who are interested in pizza lunch on day 1 or day 2 we have to submit a $5 pizza fee.
